## Iteration Detail

### Iteration 1 — Industrial Risk Comparison Table
**Hypothesis:** Add an industrial-specific red flag comparison checklist that compares environmental liability, infrastructure upgrade costs, zoning risk, insurance implications, and lease clause risks across properties.
**Target:** Red Flag Detection (3.5 → 6.0)
**Delta:** +0.65 → KEPT
**What changed:** Added "Industrial Risk Comparison" section with 7 risk categories (power infrastructure, environmental liability, fire suppression, zoning compliance, lease clause risks, insurance exposure, TI/buildout timeline), each with specific cost impacts and comparison guidance. LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH rating system.

### Iteration 2 — Requirement Compliance Matrix
**Hypothesis:** Add a requirement compliance check that systematically verifies each property against the intake requirements — power, clear height, floor load, floor drains, ventilation, compressed air, truck court, column spacing, zoning.
**Target:** Requirement Capture (5.0 → 7.0)
**Delta:** +0.90 → KEPT
**What changed:** Added "Requirement Compliance Check" section with pass/fail matrix (✅/⚠️/❌) for 11 industrial specs. Expanded Side-by-Side Specs table from 8 rows to 15, adding column spacing, floor load/slab, truck court depth, floor drains, ventilation/exhaust, compressed air, fire suppression type. Added note that ⚠️ upgrade costs must flow into Deep Cost Analysis.

### Iteration 3 — Industrial Buildout Costs
**Hypothesis:** Add industrial-specific hidden cost items to Deep Cost Analysis — transformer upgrades, ventilation/exhaust, chemical containment, fire suppression, floor drains, compressed air, CNC foundations, ITAR security, environmental permitting.
**Target:** Actionability + Industrial Specificity
**Delta:** +0.475 → KEPT
**What changed:** Added "Industrial Buildout Costs" table with 9 line items, each with typical cost range and notes. Includes transformer ($50K-$200K), ventilation ($15-$40/SF), chemical-resistant flooring ($8-$15/SF), fire suppression ($5-$15/SF), floor drains ($5K-$15K each), compressed air ($15K-$50K), CNC foundations ($3K-$10K per pad), ITAR security ($20K-$75K), environmental permitting ($10K-$30K). Added insight that lower rent + expensive upgrades can exceed higher rent move-in ready.

### Iteration 4 — Dealbreaker-First Sequencing
**Hypothesis:** Add a dealbreaker screen at the top of the comparison flow that checks 4 specs eliminating most buildings before doing the full comparison.
**Target:** Deal Efficiency + Red Flag Detection
**Initial attempt:** +0.225 → REVERTED (too thin — generic checks without industrial depth)
**Expanded attempt:** Added 5 dealbreaker checks with industrial specificity — CNC inrush current (5-8x running load), chemical zoning restrictions in industrial parks, Phase I ESA requirement for all industrial transactions, explicit timeline math (7-21 months total), and landlord insurance exclusions for chemical/hazmat operations. +0.525 → KEPT

### Iteration 5 — True Cost Ranking with NNN Reality Check
**Hypothesis:** Add a true cost ranking that re-orders properties by total occupancy cost (not asking rate) and includes NNN reality check.
**Target:** Actionability
**Initial attempt:** +0.20 → REVERTED (cost ranking alone didn't add enough specificity)
**Expanded attempt:** Added insurance differential column, permitting cost comparison, move-in timeline, NNN reconciliation warning for Houston industrial ($3-$5/SF expected vs. listed), instruction to calculate with both listed and conservative NNN estimates, specific next-step prompt at end of recommendation. +0.375 → KEPT

## Summary of Changes

The comparison skill went from a generic property comparison framework (5.25) to an industrial-specific decision engine (8.175). Key additions:

1. **Dealbreaker Screen** — 5 front-loaded checks that eliminate non-viable properties before wasting time on detailed comparison
2. **Requirement Compliance Matrix** — Pass/fail check of 11 industrial specs against the requirement document
3. **Expanded Specs Table** — 15 industrial-specific line items (up from 8 generic ones)
4. **Industrial Buildout Costs** — 9 line items with cost ranges that flow into total occupancy cost
5. **Industrial Risk Comparison** — 7 risk categories with cost impacts and LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH ratings
6. **True Cost Ranking** — Re-ranks by total occupancy cost, not asking rate; includes NNN reality check, insurance differentials, and move-in timeline
7. **Actionable Recommendation** — Calls out score vs. cost winner divergence, includes specific next-step prompt

The biggest single improvement was Iteration 2 (requirement compliance matrix, +0.90), which transformed the comparison from a subjective scoring exercise into a systematic requirements-driven evaluation.
