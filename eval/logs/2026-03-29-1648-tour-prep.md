## Summary

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Baseline total | 5.675 |
| Final total | 7.775 |
| Total improvement | +2.100 |
| Iterations kept | 4 of 5 |
| Iterations reverted | 1 (structured post-tour scorecard) |

### Improvements Kept
1. **Manufacturing & Process Infrastructure section** (+0.70) — floor drains, compressed air, slab thickness, ventilation/exhaust, chemical storage, expanded power panel details
2. **Dealbreaker-First Prioritization** (+0.70) — "First 5 Minutes" check for power, clear height, zoning, environmental red flags before full walkthrough
3. **Expensive Surprises to Watch For** (+0.40) — transformer costs, fire suppression commodity mismatch, NNN roof liability, environmental history, ITAR security, insurance implications
4. **Manufacturing-Specific Questions** (+0.30) — categorized questions covering slab thickness, discharge permits, commodity class, exclusivity clauses, relocation rights, assignment/subletting

### What Didn't Work
- **Structured post-tour scorecard** (reverted) — the scorecard improved actionability and deal efficiency conceptually, but the improvement was spread too thin across dimensions to clear the 0.3 threshold. The existing "rate on three dimensions" guidance, combined with the Property Card update instruction, provides adequate structure.

### Remaining Gaps
- **Requirement Capture (7.5):** Could still improve by dynamically generating a requirement-specific verification checklist from the Property Card rather than relying on a generic template
- **Actionability (7.5):** The after-tour output could be more structured (the reverted scorecard was directionally right but didn't score high enough)
- **Deal Efficiency (7.5):** Could add timeline awareness — e.g., "if you need to be in within 18 months and this building needs a transformer upgrade, you have 6 months left for everything else"
