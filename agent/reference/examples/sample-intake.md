# Example: Requirement Summary

*This is a sample output from the intake workflow.*

## Company
- **Company:** Apex Precision Manufacturing
- **Industry:** CNC machining and precision metal fabrication (aerospace components)
- **Employees at this location:** 45 (growing to ~70 in 18 months)
- **Special requirements:** ITAR registered, FAA Part 145 repair station. Needs secure perimeter.

## Space
- **Target size:** 40,000 – 60,000 SF
- **Clear height:** ≥ 28'
- **Office:** 10-15% (4,000-9,000 SF for engineering, QC, and admin)
- **Loading:** 2 dock-high, 2 grade-level (receives raw material on flatbeds)
- **Yard / outside storage:** Yes — minimum 0.5 acres for material staging
- **Parking:** 60 spaces minimum (1.5:1,000 ratio)

## Building Systems
- **Power:** 480V / 3-phase / minimum 2,000 amps (CNC machines draw heavy load)
- **HVAC:** Climate-controlled warehouse required (tolerance machining)
- **Crane:** 10-ton overhead bridge crane, minimum 40' span
- **Floor load:** 500 PSF (heavy equipment)
- **Fire suppression:** ESFR or wet sprinkler
- **Other utilities:** Compressed air (plant-wide), natural gas for heat treat ovens

## Location
- **Target market:** Houston, TX — northwest or west submarkets preferred
- **Proximity needs:** Within 30 min of Bush Intercontinental (IAH) for air freight; near I-10 or US-290 corridor
- **Zoning:** Heavy industrial (M-2 or equivalent)
- **Areas to avoid:** Flood-prone areas (had flood damage at current facility in 2017)

## Deal Parameters
- **Lease or buy:** Open to both. Prefer to lease 5-7 years with purchase option.
- **Budget:** $8-12/SF/yr NNN (lease) or up to $4.5M (purchase)
- **Timeline:** Need to be operational by Q1 2027 (12 months)
- **Lease term:** 5-7 years with two 5-year renewal options
- **TI needs:** Significant — need crane installation, electrical upgrade, compressed air system. Need $25-35/SF TI allowance or will negotiate longer term.

## Decision Process
- **Decision maker:** CEO (Mike Torres) and COO (Sarah Chen) — joint decision
- **Approval process:** COO narrows to 2-3 options, CEO makes final call after touring
- **Other sites under consideration:** Also looking at San Antonio (secondary market)
