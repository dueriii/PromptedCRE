## Houston Industrial Market — Q4 2025 / Q1 2026

**Vacancy:** ~6.3% overall (healthy). Small-bay and infill segments well below average — tightest in market.
**Asking Rents:**
- Market average: $10.67/SF NNN (record high, up 4.0% YoY)
- Northeast Houston (emerging): $8.00–$9.00/SF NNN
- Northwest Houston (NW): $9.00–$11.00/SF NNN
- West Houston / Energy Corridor (premium): $11.00–$13.00/SF NNN
- Small-bay (<25K SF, quality infill): $12.00–$15.00/SF NNN

**Supply:** 18.6 MSF delivered in 2025. 14.4 MSF still under construction. 43.7% of 2025 deliveries pre-leased — demand is real.
**Available inventory:** 83.7 MSF (includes sublet + soon-to-be-vacant). Up slightly from Q3.
**Cap Rates:** 5.5%–7.5% for stabilized NNN industrial. Single-tenant, credit tenant: 5.5%–6.5%. Older vintage / metal construction: 7.0%–8.5%.

**Submarket Notes:**
- Northwest Houston: Active development (W. Little York corridor). Institutional demand.
- Southwest Houston / Chocolate Bayou: Growing. I-69 access. Manufacturing/distribution focus.
- Northeast Houston: Emerging. Cheaper land. Longer lease-up risk.
- Ship Channel / Southeast: Heavy industrial, logistics. Tight vacancy.

## San Antonio / I-35 Corridor (Schertz / New Braunfels / Cibolo) — Q4 2025 / Q1 2026

**Vacancy:** Near-term rebalancing. Elevated vs. 2022-2023 peaks but fundamentals sound.
**Asking Rents:**
- Schertz / I-35 North: $9.00–$12.00/SF NNN
- New Braunfels (newer product 2023-2026): $12.96–$15.00/SF NNN
- North SA (Loop 1604 / 281): $8.50–$11.00/SF NNN

**Demand Drivers:** I-35 corridor is a major logistics artery (SA ↔ Austin ↔ DFW). Schertz/Cibolo/New Braunfels absorbing hundreds of millions in new industrial development. $450M+ robotic DC announced for Cibolo area. Military-adjacent demand (JBSA).

**Cap Rates:** 7.0%–9.0% for older vintage. 6.0%–7.5% for newer NNN credit deals.

**Submarket Notes:**
- Schertz / I-35: HOT. Institutional-grade supply coming. Re-lease risk low for well-located assets.
- North SA (Loop 1604): Largest absorption share. Strong tenant demand from manufacturing/logistics.
- Far South SA (Hwy 281 South): Light industrial, growing but thinner tenant pool.
